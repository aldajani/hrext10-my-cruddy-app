Perfect Playlist

Welcome to Perfect Playlist! Save all your favorite songs to a playlist that you can easily update, edit, or add to with the touch of a button. Built for those serious about their music listening experience.

Getting Started

To start building your perfect playlist, simply input the song and artist name into the appropriate fields, and then click on the "Add Song" button. If you want to update the information for a song, simply enter the song name into the "song" field, update the corresponding information, and then click "Update". You can also delete a song from your playlist by entering the song name and clicking the "Delete Song" button.
